Celestial Suites Brand

Brand Design

Based on the Celestials race in the MMORPG MapleStory, Celestial Suites is an international luxury hotel and resort company that operates hotels around the world that desires to provide guests with personalized accommodations in practicing their religion, including providing bibles.

The Celestial Suites Brand represents the symbol of professionalism & luxury while maintaining their warm welcomes to everyone, regardless of religion.

The logo’s circular design represents the mythical Celestials’ angelic halo, those who serve their Goddess, encircling the center diamond, representing the people the Celestials protect, along with guests they accommodate & luxury they contain.

Stationery Design

Celestial Suite's stationery consists of their business card, letterhead, envelope & pencils. With the Mitra Goddess illustration as the centrepiece, it represents the brand's symbolic devotion to not discriminate and maintain their open arms to all, regardless of religion.

Advertisement Design

An outdoor billboard near various airports & churches defining Celestial Suites' focus to luxurious services and religious accomodations to bring in religious travelers.
