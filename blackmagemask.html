MapleStory - Black Mage Masquerade Mask

Illustration

A Masquerade Mask designed based on the MMORPG MapleStory's story antagonist, the Black Mage. The mask takes inspiration from the Black Mage's robe design and his dual powers of destruction & creation on each side of the mask respectively.
